# TBAO Principles / TBAO 核心原则

> Status / 状态: v0.1 public draft / v0.1 公开草稿

---

## 1. Human Meaning Sovereignty / 人的意义主权

**English**

Human beings retain final authority over the meaning of their experience, knowledge, preferences, style, goals, and responsibility boundaries.

AI may assist interpretation, but it must not silently replace human meaning with inferred meaning.

**中文**

人对自身经验、知识、偏好、风格、目标和责任边界保留最终解释权。

AI 可以辅助解释，但不得悄悄用推断意义替代人的意义。

---

## 2. Maximize Cognition, Bound Action / 最大化认知自由，约束行动权力

**English**

AI should be free to think, analyze, draft, simulate, critique, and explore in safe spaces.

But every action that changes durable state, external systems, messages, money, credentials, memory, code history, or production environments must enter governance.

**中文**

AI 可以在安全空间中自由思考、分析、草拟、模拟、质疑和探索。

但凡会改变长期状态、外部系统、消息、资金、密钥、记忆、代码历史或生产环境的行动，都必须进入治理链路。

---

## 3. Provenance Before Reuse / 复用之前先确认来源

**English**

Human knowledge, memory, and preference must retain source, time, confidence, confirmation status, and actionability before being reused for advice or action.

**中文**

人的知识、记忆和偏好，在被用于建议或行动之前，必须保留来源、时间、置信度、确认状态和可行动边界。

---

## 4. Advice Must Be Challengeable / 建议必须可质疑

**English**

Any output that significantly shapes human judgment should be source-grounded, challengeable, verifiable, and downgradeable.

Persuasive language is not evidence.

**中文**

任何会显著影响人类判断的输出，都应可溯源、可质疑、可校验、可降级。

有说服力的语言不是证据。

---

## 5. Side-Effectful Action Requires Structure / 有副作用行动必须结构化

**English**

An agent should not jump directly from intent to execution.

Side-effectful action must pass through structured request, risk classification, policy decision, execution guard, and ledger record.

**中文**

Agent 不应从意图直接跳到执行。

有外部副作用的行动必须经过结构化请求、风险分级、策略裁决、执行守卫和账本记录。

---

## 6. Human Approval Must Not Be Theater / 人在环不能形式化

**English**

Human-in-the-loop must not become a decorative confirmation button.

For medium-risk and high-risk actions, the human should see what is being done, why it is being done, what evidence supports it, what can go wrong, and how it can be stopped, rolled back, or reviewed.

**中文**

人在环不能变成装饰性的确认按钮。

对于中高风险动作，人应看到：要做什么、为什么做、依据是什么、可能出什么问题，以及如何停止、回滚或复盘。

---

## 7. Recoverability Is a Governance Requirement / 可恢复性是治理要求

**English**

A governance spine is incomplete if it cannot support stop, rollback, quarantine, replay, incident note, or responsibility mapping where appropriate.

**中文**

如果一个治理脊柱不能在适当场景下支持停止、回滚、隔离、重放、事故记录或责任映射，它就是不完整的。

---

## 8. Completion Is Not the Highest Objective / 完成任务不是最高目标

**English**

Task completion must not override meaning preservation, boundary integrity, evidence traceability, and recoverability.

**中文**

完成任务不能凌驾于意义保护、边界完整、证据可追踪和可恢复性之上。

---

## 9. Constraint Must Be Structural / 约束必须是结构性的

**English**

A safety rule that depends only on the agent remembering to behave is not enough.

Governance should be represented in objects, flows, policies, logs, approvals, guards, and recovery interfaces.

**中文**

只依赖 Agent 自己记得要守规矩的安全规则是不够的。

治理应体现在对象、流程、策略、日志、审批、守卫和恢复接口之中。

---

## 10. Final Judgment Remains Human / 最终裁决权仍归人

**English**

TBAO can support decision quality, traceability, and risk control. It does not replace human responsibility, law, institutional authority, or moral judgment.

**中文**

TBAO 可以支持决策质量、可追溯性和风险控制，但不替代人的责任、法律、组织授权或道德判断。
